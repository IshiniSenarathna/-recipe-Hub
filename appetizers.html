Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Toast the baguette slices until crispy.
  2. In a bowl, mix tomatoes, garlic, chopped basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Top the toasted bread with the tomato mixture.
  4. Serve immediately while bread is still warm.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and finely chop them.
  3. In a bowl, mix cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, parsley, and chopped stems.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stuff mushroom caps with the mixture and place on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
