Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate in a double boiler or microwave. Let cool slightly.
  2. Beat egg yolks with sugar until thick and pale. Stir in vanilla and melted chocolate.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip the cream until soft peaks form. Fold into chocolate mixture.
  4. Beat egg whites with a pinch of salt until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into the mousse.
  5. Spoon into serving dishes and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Apple Crumble

Ingredients:

  • 4 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup butter, chilled and diced
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Place apples in a greased baking dish. Sprinkle with a little cinnamon and nutmeg.
  3. In a bowl, mix flour, oats, brown sugar, and remaining cinnamon.
  4. Cut in the butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. Sprinkle topping evenly over apples.
  6. Bake for 35–40 minutes until golden and bubbly. Serve warm with ice cream if desired.
